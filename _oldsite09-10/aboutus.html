University of California, Berkeley steel bridge team started in 2000 .... More to come!

 

The Steel Bridge Competition invites schools from across the nation to create a scale bridge to fit annual specifications. Teams not only design, test and fabricate a bridge, but they also compete for fastest construction time and efficiency. The competitions take place on a regional and national level.

Each year, there are unique specifications for the bridge, its method of construction, and scoring distribution. More information about the competition can be found at www.AISC.org under Student Competitions.
